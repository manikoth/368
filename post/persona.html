Web Design 1

P1: Persona

See quick-screencast first for details.

Objectives

  • Start the visual design process
  • A collage or moodboard is the first step towards a Style Tile
  • Introduce Adobe Photoshop into the workflow

Content

  1. Go to the backend of your user.github.io repo, e.g. github.com/user
  2. Create a wiki page named persona
  3. Using Markdown syntax, address the following:
    • What nouns (person, place, thing) best describes you and/or your body of work?
    • If you had an aesthetic - colors, textures, patterns, etc. - what would it look it?
    • What nouns would not be descriptive of your identity (brand)?

Design

  1. Clone your user.github.io repo
  2. Download persona template & add to psd folder
  3. Create a collage 8.5x11 at 72 dpi in Adobe Photoshop
  4. From your persona wiki, Add images, text, and other elements that are indicative of your indentity (branding)
  5. No "lime green" background should be visible. Use layer stacking, resizing, cropping, and the wand tool to cut elements.
  6. Save to psd folder. Commit changes. Push to GitHub.

Submission

  • On Canvas, submit URL the backend of your user.github.io repo, e.g. github.com/user
